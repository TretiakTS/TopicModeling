Topic Modeling

Introduction:

After trying the most popular topic modeling tools, the best performance was observed using the NMF model.

It appeared to be:

  • easy to implement
  • the topics are distinguishing and mostly consist of useful medical terms
  • the distribution of documents between the topics is quite equal
  • fast in loading

Topics created by the model:

  1. Antibiotic resistance
  2. Bacteria
  3. Fungal
  4. Biofilms
  5. Mutation
