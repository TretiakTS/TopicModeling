Topic Modeling

Introduction:

This web-application was created in order to provide the details of the results of my research connected to finding the best suitable topic modeling tool for the given biomedical data and to give the opportunity to assign created topics to new beomedical documents. Topic modeling is one of the popular methods among text mining techniques used to discover hidden topics. The massive amount of biomedical text data can be a valuable source of knowledge for biomedical research. Therefore, there is a need to produce efficient topic modeling techniques to discover hidden topics in complex biomedical texts.

The aim was to find the strategy which gives the opportunity to create the most unique and meaningful topics for document classification in addition to creating a minimal number of outliers and distributing documents quite equally .

In my work I tried the most popular models like LDA, NMF, Bertopic and Top2Vec. For data preprocessing I used the SciSpacy library for stopwords and lemmatization.
